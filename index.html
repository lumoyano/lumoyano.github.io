About me

Hello! I am Lucas, a young passionate software developer with a strong foundation in Java. With my recent Software Engineering degree (A.S. plus Certificate) from Ensign College, I am dedicated to constantly push myself to become a valuable problem solver in the Technology world.

My journey in the world of technology began with a curiosity for Web development at age 12. Ten Years later I have developed sharp problem solving skills, an extensive knowledge of Java, SQL, Front-end Web Dev, Agile Methodologies, familiarity with multiple languages and frameworks, and the versatility to keep up with multiple projects in different fields. In 2 years of high-quality Tech Support Experience I have consistently proven a love for delivering real results, and applied skills and experience in developing standardized trainings used to instruct new hires on Windows 10 Diagnostics and Troubleshooting.

I thrive in social and collaborative environments and am always eager to face new challenges. My goal is to break into the fields of Tech and Cloud, and grow my passion and understanding alongside my more seasoned peers.
